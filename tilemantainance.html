Tile Maintenance Tips

Keep your tiles looking new with these simple maintenance tips.

  • Sweep or vacuum daily to remove dust and debris.
  • Use a damp mop with a mild detergent for deeper cleaning.
  • Avoid abrasive cleaning tools to prevent scratches.

  • Use a baking soda paste for tough stains.
  • Hydrogen peroxide works well for stubborn stains on ceramic tiles.
  • For grease stains, clean with warm water and dish soap.

  • Clean grout regularly using a vinegar and water solution.
  • Reapply grout sealer every 6-12 months to prevent staining.
  • For deep cleaning, use a grout brush and baking soda paste.

  • Use furniture pads to avoid scratches.
  • Place doormats at entrances to reduce dirt accumulation.
  • Avoid dragging heavy objects directly on tiles.
